I am a PhD student at the University of Bath under the supervision of Xiuping Su. Our research is centred around cluster algebras on Grassmannians and their quantum counterparts. In fact any bit of maths containing the word Grassmannian is probably of great interest to me. The two main things I'm thinking about at the moment are quantum cluster characters for Grassmannians as well as interesting questions about quiver Grassmannians. Once I have some papers to display I will add a section for these below.
