I am a PhD student at the University of Bath under the supervision of Xiuping Su. My research is centred around cluster algebras on Grassmannians, their quantum counterparts and the role of quiver Grassmannians in this picture. In fact any bit of maths containing the word Grassmannian is probably of great interest to me. Once I have some papers to display I will add a section for these below.
